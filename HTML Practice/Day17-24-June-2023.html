Combine Diacritical Mark

What is that?

Diacritial is some character like '̀ ' , '́ ' , '̂ ' '̃ ' , '̄ ' , '̅ ' which are called Mark.
You can combine Mark and character like à , b́ , ĉ etc...

How to use it?

You can enter the number like &#768; or &#x0300; and so on.
If the unit is decimal, the range of Mark is 768 to 879.
If Hexdecimal, the range of Mark is 0300 to 036F.
